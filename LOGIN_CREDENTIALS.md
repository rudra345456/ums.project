## Login Instructions
### For Teachers:
1. Click on 'Login' button
2. Select 'Teacher Login'
3. Enter your **Email** (e.g., aditya.gautam@gla.edu)
4. Enter your **Password** (e.g., teacher001)
5. Click 'Login'

### For Students:
1. Click on 'Login' button
2. Select 'Student Login'
3. Enter your **Email** (e.g., aarav.sharma@student.gla.edu)
4. Enter your **Password** (e.g., student001)
5. Click 'Login'

## Teacher Capabilities
- Upload attendance for their subject
- Create assignments with document upload
- Grade assignments submitted by students
- Upload marks after exam completion
- View and update timetable for their specific subject

## Student Capabilities
- Upload assignments
- View attendance (updated when teacher uploads it)
- View timetable
- View grades and marks
